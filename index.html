About Me

My name is Anne Sophie da Silva. I was born in France, lived in Spain for 8 years, and I have been living in Braga, Portugal for 14 years. I hold a bachelor's degree in Applied Biology from the University of Minho and a master's degree in Ecology from the same institution.

In the beginning of 2023, I decided to undergo a professional reconversion, so I ventured into the world of programming. I enrolled in a Software Developer course from CESAE Digital, which consisted of 4 months of classes and a 3-month internship.
I completed my internship at NKA -New Knowledge Advice where I actively contributed to the development and programming of an application and web pages using JavaScript and the REACT library, gaining valuable hands-on experience in modern web development technologies.

Knowledge
